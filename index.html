about
Based in Seoul, NCT 127 is a sub-unit of another group called NCT, whose name stands for Neo Culture Technology. “127” refers to the longitudinal coordinate of this sub-unit’s home city.
NCT is the abbreviation of ‘Neo Culture Technology’. As it is based on new cultural technology, it has an entirely new concept of group with ‘openness’ and ‘expandability’ as their main points, which able adding new members and having no limit on the number of members. Under the brand, NCT, each team based on the cities around the world will make their debut in sequence.
Sticker is the third Korean-language and fourth overall studio album by South Korean boy band NCT 127. It was released on September 17, 2021, by SM Entertainment through Dreamus in South Korea and Virgin Music Label & Artist Services in United States. It follows their previous release, the Japanese-language EP Loveholic, released in February 2021, and their previous Korean studio album Neo Zone, released in March 2020.
